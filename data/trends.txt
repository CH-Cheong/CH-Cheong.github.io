Major Research Trends 

2025–2026
-On-device LLM evaluation in the building-environment domain
-Construction of domain-specific QA benchmarks and dataset quality assessment
-AI-assisted research workflows and automation in architectural research

2024–2025
-Urban-scale energy reduction strategies and building energy prediction models
-Framework development for healing environments
-Adaptive ventilation strategies in modular and single-occupancy units

2021–2023
-Ventilation optimization in classrooms and educational facilities
-Airborne infection probability modeling under occupancy scenarios
-Integration of hybrid mechanical ventilation and airflow control systems

2018–2020
-CFD-based airborne pathogen dispersion analysis in hospitals and emergency departments
-Application of Wells–Riley equation for infection risk prediction
-Environmental health and quality-of-life assessment studies

2014–2017
-Energy-efficient residential system development (double windows, PV integration, airtightness improvement)
-Thermal comfort and daylighting performance evaluation
-Early exploration of infection control through environmental design
